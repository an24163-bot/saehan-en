IN-HOUSE R&D INSTITUTE

R&D-Driven Innovation
Since 2000

Saehan Nanotech has operated a certified in-house research institute since 2009, continuously developing proprietary technologies in precision machine tool design and hard brittle material processing.

Over the past two decades, we have successfully completed multiple government-funded national R&D projects — validating our technology at the highest level and building deep expertise across semiconductor, display, energy, and defense applications.

BUSINESS

Business Overview

From product development to after-sales service — everything in-house.

WHAT WE DO

Semiconductor & Display
Equipment Manufacturing

Saehan Nanotech is a precision machine tool manufacturer specializing in equipment for semiconductor, display, and advanced material processing. We directly handle the entire lifecycle — from product development and mechanical design to CNC manufacturing, assembly, installation, operator training, and after-sales service.

No outsourced engineering. No third-party service contractors. Every machine is built, tested, and supported by the same team that designed it.

END-TO-END CAPABILITY

Full In-House Process

Every step is performed by Saehan Nanotech's own engineering and production team.

01

Product Development

Customer requirements analysis, feasibility study, concept design, and prototype validation — all driven by our in-house R&D team.

02

Mechanical Design

3D CAD modeling, structural analysis (FEA), tolerance stack-up, and design-for-manufacturing. Every component engineered for precision and reliability.

03

CNC Manufacturing

In-house CNC machining center for all critical components. Precision grinding, turning, and milling to sub-micron tolerances.

04

Assembly & Testing

Machine assembly, alignment, calibration, and full performance testing at our Changwon factory before delivery.

05

Installation & Training

On-site installation, process recipe development, and hands-on operator training — we don't leave until your team is running production.

06

After-Sales Service

Direct engineer support from the team that built your machine. Remote diagnostics, spare parts, preventive maintenance, and process optimization.

INDUSTRIES SERVED

Where Our Machines Work

⚡

Semiconductor

Silicon electrode drilling, SiC wafer slicing, quartz and AlN component machining for etch, CVD, and ALD process chambers.

📱

Display & LED

Nano-imprint lithography for Nano-LED and micro-LED patterning. Sapphire substrate slicing for LED epitaxial layers.

🚗

EV & Automotive

NdFeB rare-earth permanent magnet slicing for EV traction motors. SiC power device substrate processing.

💨

Energy & Defense

Rare-earth magnet processing for wind turbine generators. Diamond wire sawing for defense industry applications.

WHO WE ARE

Precision Machine Tools
for Critical Materials

Saehan Nanotech is a precision machine tool manufacturer specializing in equipment for ultra-precision machining of hard and brittle materials — the foundational substrates that underpin the semiconductor, display, defense, and clean energy industries.

We handle the entire value chain in-house: product development, mechanical and control system design, manufacturing, assembly, quality testing, installation, operator training, and after-sales service. No outsourcing. No third-party contractors. Every machine that leaves our factory is built, tested, and supported by the same engineering team.

INDUSTRIES SERVED

Machining the Materials That Power Industries

Our equipment processes the foundational substrates and precision components at the core of each industry — where micron-level accuracy determines product performance.

🔬

Semiconductor

Silicon electrodes, SiC power device substrates, quartz chamber components, AlN heat sinks — the consumable and structural parts inside semiconductor fab equipment.

Si SiC Quartz AlN
📱

Display

Nano-LED and micro-LED patterning substrates, sapphire wafers for LED, and precision optical glass components for next-generation display manufacturing.

Sapphire Nano-LED Glass
🛡️

Defense

Precision-cut substrates for advanced sensor systems, infrared optics, and specialized ceramic components used in defense and national security applications.

IR Optics Ceramics Sensors
⚡

EV & Clean Energy

NdFeB and SmCo rare earth permanent magnets for EV traction motors and wind turbine generators. SiC substrates for power semiconductor devices.

NdFeB SmCo SiC Power

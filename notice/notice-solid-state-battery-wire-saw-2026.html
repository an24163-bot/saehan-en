Saehan Nanotech Completes Development of Diamond Single Wire Sawing Machine for All-Solid-State Battery Electrode Processing

Precision shape processing of next-generation solid-state battery electrodes — minimized kerf loss and material damage

Saehan Nanotech is pleased to announce the successful development of our Diamond Single Wire Sawing Machine, purpose-built for cutting and shape processing of all-solid-state battery materials.

The all-solid-state battery is widely regarded as the next frontier of energy storage — eliminating liquid electrolytes to deliver higher energy density, faster charging, and improved safety.

Diamond wire sawing for precision electrode processing

The machine is designed to cut solid-state battery electrodes to the precise dimensions required for cell stack assembly. Diamond wire sawing technology is well suited to the multi-layered, sensitive structures of solid-state battery materials, delivering consistent surface quality and dimensional accuracy.

Saehan Nanotech configures each unit to match the customer's cell design, production environment, and volume requirements — custom engineering is available on request. For detailed specifications or to discuss your application, please contact us directly.

Key highlights

  • ✅ Dry cutting process — eliminates coolant contamination
  • ✅ Minimized kerf loss and material damage to battery cell materials
  • ✅ Optimized cutting dimensions for all-solid-state battery electrode assembly
  • ✅ Commissioning test successfully completed

Over 20 years of independently developed diamond wire sawing

Saehan Nanotech has independently developed and manufactured precision diamond wire sawing machines for over 20 years. Our diamond wire sawing lineup includes single-wire and multi-wire systems, capable of cutting disks, rings, and other specialty shapes.

The series has been supplied to customers in the semiconductor, display, and rare-earth permanent magnet industries across Korea, China, Malaysia, Vietnam, and the United States. In response to the rapidly growing demand from the all-solid-state battery sector, Saehan Nanotech has now completed development of a model purpose-built for this emerging application.

Every machine has been designed and manufactured at our Changwon, Korea headquarters and factory since the company's founding in 2000, with after-sales service handled directly by our in-house team.

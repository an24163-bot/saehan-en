Saehan Nanotech is Hiring Experienced Engineers for Machine Tool and Semiconductor Equipment Design

Saehan Nanotech Co., Ltd. is hiring experienced engineers for machine tool and semiconductor equipment design positions at our Changwon headquarters.

Open Positions

  • Mechanical Design Engineer — Precision machine tool structure design, spindle system design, fixturing design. 3+ years experience with SolidWorks or similar CAD.
  • Control System Engineer — CNC controller integration (FANUC), servo motor control, PLC programming. Experience with semiconductor equipment preferred.
  • Process Engineer — Machining process development for hard brittle materials (Si, SiC, Sapphire). Recipe optimization and customer application support.

Why Join Saehan Nanotech

We design, manufacture, and service all our machines in-house. As an engineer at Saehan Nanotech, you will be involved in the complete lifecycle — from concept design through manufacturing, testing, installation, and customer support. This is a rare opportunity to work across the full engineering spectrum in a growing company.

To apply, please send your resume to info@saehannanotech.com with the subject line "Engineer Application".

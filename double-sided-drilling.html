ANT-BSSD500 Both-Side Drilling Machine

BOTH SIDES SPINDLE DRILLING MACHINE

ANT-BSSD500

Both-Side Drilling Machine

The world's first simultaneous dual-spindle micro hole drilling machine. Two spindles drill from both faces at once — eliminating the workpiece reversal step that causes concentricity defects in conventional drilling. Horizontal structure enables natural chip evacuation for deep-hole drilling impossible with vertical machines.

  • 01Both-side simultaneous drilling — zero repositioning, concentricity within 20㎛
  • 02Horizontal structure — natural chip evacuation enables deep hole (L/D 30) drilling
  • 032~3× throughput vs vertical type with optimized recipe — extended unattended run time

KEY ADVANTAGES

Technical Highlights

01

Both-Side Simultaneous Drilling

Both spindles drill simultaneously — zero re-fixturing error. Concentricity ≤ 20㎛ across 100% of holes. Single uninterrupted machining cycle.

02

Horizontal Machining Structure

Gravity-assisted chip evacuation. Clean chip path keeps coolant open. L/D up to 30 validated. 2~3× throughput vs vertical type.

03

Peck Drilling Optimization

Automatic peck drilling cycle with programmable retract depth, dwell time, and feed rate. Optimized chip breaking prevents packing in deep holes.

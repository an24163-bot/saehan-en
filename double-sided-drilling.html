ANT-BSSD500

Both-Side Drilling Machine

The world's first simultaneous dual-spindle micro hole drilling machine. Two spindles drill from both faces at once — eliminating the workpiece reversal that causes concentricity defects in conventional drilling.

  • 01Both-side simultaneous drilling — concentricity within 20㎛
  • 02Horizontal structure — natural chip evacuation, L/D 30
  • 032~3× throughput vs vertical type — extended unattended run time

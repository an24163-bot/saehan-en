ANT-BSSD500

Both-Side Drilling Machine

The world's first simultaneous dual-spindle micro hole drilling machine. Two spindles drill from both faces at once — eliminating the workpiece reversal step that causes concentricity defects in conventional drilling. Horizontal structure enables natural chip evacuation for deep-hole drilling impossible with vertical machines.

DIFFERENTIATION

2 Design Innovations That Solve Real Problems

Both-side drilling and horizontal structure improve quality, machinability, and productivity simultaneously.

01

Both-Side Simultaneous Drilling

Eliminates workpiece reversal — improves concentricity and reduces defects

BEFORE

Single-Side Type

  • Two-step process: machine one side, flip and re-fixture
  • Pitch error and concentricity defects from reversal
  • Longer cycle time, operator intervention required
AFTER

Both-Side Type

  • Both spindles drill simultaneously — no reversal
  • Concentricity ≤ 20㎛ maintained consistently
  • Extended unattended machining — yield and productivity maximized
02

Horizontal Machining Structure

Natural chip evacuation along horizontal hole — enables deep hole drilling

BEFORE

Vertical Type

  • Gravity causes chips to accumulate inside the hole
  • Poor coolant evacuation — drill breakage risk
  • Deep hole machining limited
AFTER

Horizontal Type

  • Natural chip evacuation along horizontal axis
  • 1.5~2× throughput under same conditions
  • Optimized recipe: 2~3× productivity achieved

ANT-BSSD500 Both-Side Drilling Machine

BOTH SIDES SPINDLE DRILLING MACHINE

ANT-BSSD500

The world's first simultaneous dual-spindle micro hole drilling machine — designed from the ground up to solve the concentricity defect problem in semiconductor electrode manufacturing. Where conventional machines flip the workpiece and introduce alignment errors, the ANT-BSSD500 drills both faces at once, eliminating that error source entirely.

PATENTED INNOVATION

Two Design Innovations That Solve Real Problems

Korean Patent No. 10-1579164 — Silicon Cathode Micro Drilling Device

01

Both-Side Simultaneous Drilling

Both spindles drill simultaneously — zero repositioning, zero re-fixturing error. Concentricity maintained at 20㎛ or better across 100% of holes. Single uninterrupted machining cycle with extended unattended run time.

02

Horizontal Machining Structure

Horizontal axis aligns with gravity-assisted chip evacuation — chips exit the hole naturally. Clean chip evacuation keeps coolant path open. Aspect ratio (L/D) up to 30 validated. 2~3× throughput vs vertical type.

03

Peck Drilling Optimization

Automatic peck drilling cycle with programmable retract depth, dwell time, and feed rate. Optimized chip breaking strategy prevents chip packing in deep holes — extending tool life and maintaining hole quality.

NANO IMPRINTING

Nano Imprinting Machine

The Saehan Nanotech Nano Imprinting Machine enables the creation of nanoscale surface structures on semiconductor and display substrates through precision imprinting. Nano-imprint lithography (NIL) is a cost-effective alternative to photolithography for creating periodic and aperiodic nanopatterns — with applications in LED efficiency enhancement, anti-reflection surfaces, and advanced semiconductor device structures.

01

Sub-100nm Pattern Resolution

Using precision-engineered molds and controlled pressure/UV/thermal conditions, the machine transfers patterns with feature sizes below 100nm — enabling next-generation optical and electronic device structures.

02

UV and Thermal Imprinting Modes

Compatible with both UV-curable resist (room temperature, faster cycle time) and thermal imprinting (higher temperature, compatible with a wider range of substrate materials). Process mode selectable per application.

03

Uniform Pressure Control

Servo-controlled pressure system ensures uniform imprinting force across the entire substrate area — critical for defect-free pattern transfer across large substrates or when working near the edge of the mold.

Both Side Spindle Drilling Machine · ANT-BSSD500

The World's First Both-Side Simultaneous Drilling

Our machines are highly specialized for semiconductor parts. The Both-Side Spindle Drilling Machine (ANT-BSSD500) creates tiny holes in silicon electrodes and other semiconductor components simultaneously from both sides — eliminating the workpiece flipping step that causes alignment errors and defects.

01

Both-Side Simultaneous Drilling

Two spindles drill simultaneously from both sides, eliminating the workpiece reversal step. Concentricity maintained within 20μm at all times.

02

Horizontal Structure

Horizontal machining axis enables natural chip evacuation by gravity, preventing chip buildup inside holes. Enables deep hole (L/D up to 30) drilling not possible with vertical machines.

03

Patented Technology

Developed entirely in-house and protected by patent (No. 10-1579164). No other manufacturer offers simultaneous dual-sided drilling for semiconductor electrode components.

Application

Which semiconductor parts does it machine?

Consumable components used in dry etch, CVD, and ALD processes require hundreds to thousands of precision holes. Our machine is specialized for these applications.

Silicon Electrode

SILICON ELECTRODE

Plasma-generating electrode inside dry etch chambers. Requires hundreds to thousands of precision through-holes on both faces.

Key Application

Shower Head

SHOWER HEAD

Gas distribution component in CVD/ALD chambers. Requires high-precision hole arrays for uniform gas distribution.

Key Application

Focus Ring

FOCUS RING

Wafer edge protection during etch processes. Requires precision pin holes and assembly holes in SiC material.

SiC Specialist

Susceptor

SUSCEPTOR

Wafer support and heating component. Requires vacuum hole and lift-pin hole arrays.

SiC / AlN

Wafer Chuck / ESC

WAFER CHUCK / ESC

Electrostatic chuck with thousands of micro vacuum holes. Ultra-precision hole machining in AlN and SiC.

AlN Specialist

Quartz Ring / Parts

QUARTZ RING / PARTS

Precision machining of assembly holes and sensor holes in fused silica components for high-temperature processes.

Differentiation

Beyond the Limits of Conventional Machining

Two patented design innovations — both-side simultaneous drilling and horizontal machining structure — work together to deliver unmatched quality, machinability, and productivity.

01 · Both-Side Simultaneous Drilling — Eliminates Workpiece Reversal

BEFORE — Single-Direction Type

2-step process: machine one face, then flip and re-fixture the workpiece

Reversal step causes pitch error and concentricity defects

Longer cycle time with mandatory operator intervention → higher labor cost

Yield losses from alignment errors

AFTER — Both-Side Type (ANT-BSSD500)

Both spindles drill simultaneously — no workpiece reversal needed

Concentricity maintained at 20μm or less at all times

Minimizes hole quality impact even when machine pitch error occurs

Increased unattended machining time — higher yield and productivity

02 · Horizontal Machining Structure — Enables Deep Hole Drilling

BEFORE — Vertical Type

Gravity causes chip accumulation inside holes

Poor coolant evacuation → risk of drill breakage

Limited capability for deep hole drilling

AFTER — Horizontal Type (ANT-BSSD500)

Natural chip evacuation along horizontal axis — protects drills

1.5~2× productivity improvement under equivalent conditions

2~3× productivity with optimized recipe

Aspect ratio (L/D) up to 30 tested and validated

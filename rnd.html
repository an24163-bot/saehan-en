IN-HOUSE RESEARCH INSTITUTE

Dedicated R&D Since 2009

Saehan Nanotech operates a certified in-house research institute focused on precision machining technology for hard brittle materials. Our R&D covers machine design, process optimization, tooling development, and new material application research.

Research Milestones

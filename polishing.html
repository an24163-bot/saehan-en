STEP POLISHING

Single Polishing Machine

The Saehan Nanotech Single Polishing Machine (also called Step Polishing Machine or Flat Polishing Machine) is specialized for precision polishing of non-standard surface geometries. While most polishing machines handle flat faces, this machine is engineered to achieve mirror finish on stepped surfaces, inclined planes, and groove structures — features that are common in semiconductor electrode components but extremely challenging to polish uniformly.

01

Step and Groove Polishing Specialty

Uniquely capable of achieving consistent surface finish across stepped transitions and groove profiles. The controlled pressure and kinematics prevent rounding of sharp edges that would compromise component function.

02

Mirror-Finish Capability

Achieves surface roughness Ra < 1nm (mirror finish) on hard materials including silicon, SiC, and engineering ceramics. Uses precision lapping film or polishing pad with slurry appropriate to the material.

03

Customizable Process Parameters

Rotation speed, pressure, oscillation pattern, and abrasive selection are all independently controlled and saved as recipes — enabling repeatable results across production batches.

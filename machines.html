Machine Overview

Our complete lineup of ultra-precision machine tools for semiconductor and display component processing.

Product Lineup

Total 8 Machines

From micro hole drilling to wire sawing, grinding, polishing, and nano imprinting — a complete solution for precision semiconductor component machining.

01
MICRO DRILLING

Both-Side Drilling Machine

Simultaneous dual-spindle micro hole drilling for silicon electrodes, shower heads, and other semiconductor components. Concentricity within 20μm.

View Details
02
WIRE SAWING

Multi Wire Sawing Machine

Multi-wire diamond wire sawing for mass production cutting of Si, SiC, Sapphire, Quartz, ALN, Graphite, and magnetic materials.

View Details
03
WIRE SAWING

Single Wire Sawing Machine

Single diamond loop wire sawing with freely adjustable kerf and thickness. Ideal for R&D and small-batch precision cutting.

View Details
04
GRINDING

Grinding Center Machine

Multi-axis CNC grinding center for complex surface profiles, ID/OD grinding of hard brittle semiconductor and ceramic materials.

View Details
05
CORE DRILLING

Core Drilling Machine

Cylindrical coring of semiconductor ingots (Si, SiC, Sapphire) to produce tubular workpieces for downstream processing.

View Details
06
STEP POLISHING

Single Polishing Machine

Precision mirror-finish polishing of step surfaces, inclined planes, and groove structures in hard ceramic and semiconductor materials.

View Details
07
VERTICAL GRIND

Vertical Grinding Machine

Vertical-axis grinding for high-precision flat surface processing of large-diameter semiconductor and ceramic workpieces.

View Details
08
NANO IMPRINT

Nano Imprinting Machine

Nano-scale pattern imprinting on semiconductor and display substrates. Creates sub-100nm structures for advanced device fabrication.

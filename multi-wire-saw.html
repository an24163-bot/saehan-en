Company History

Our journey of innovation in precision semiconductor machine tool manufacturing.

Our Journey

Building Precision, Year by Year

FOUNDATION

Saehan Nanotech Co., Ltd. Established

Founded in Changwon, Gyeongsangnam-do, with a clear focus on precision machine tool manufacturing for the semiconductor industry. Started with core competencies in wire sawing and drilling technology.

EARLY DEVELOPMENT

Multi Wire Sawing Machine Development

Developed and commercialized multi-wire sawing machines capable of cutting hard brittle materials including Si, SiC, Sapphire, Quartz, and ALN with world-class precision.

PATENT

Both-Side Drilling Machine Patent Registration

Secured patent protection for the Both-Side Spindle Drilling Machine (Patent No. 10-1579164) — the world's first simultaneous dual-spindle micro hole drilling machine for silicon electrodes. A technological breakthrough that eliminates workpiece flipping and dramatically improves concentricity.

EXPANSION

SNTECH Affiliate Established

Established SNTECH (에스앤테크) as an affiliated company to handle precision machining operations, creating a vertically integrated ecosystem for semiconductor component processing from machine manufacturing to actual machining services.

RECOGNITION

Selected for National R&D Program — Nano-LED Display

Saehan Nanotech was selected as a host company for the government-backed Nano-LED Display R&D national project, validating our role at the forefront of next-generation display and semiconductor technology development.

TODAY

Continued Growth in SiC & Advanced Materials

Expanding our capabilities to address the growing demand for SiC (Silicon Carbide) component machining driven by the electric vehicle and power semiconductor markets. Ongoing R&D on AlN, quartz, and next-generation ceramic materials.

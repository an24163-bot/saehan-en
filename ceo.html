Message from CEO

Committed to Ultra-Precision Innovation

AN
An Guk-jin
CEO

Dear valued customers and partners,

Thank you for visiting Saehan Nanotech. We specialize in the research, development, and manufacturing of ultra-precision machine tools — particularly for the semiconductor and display industries.

Our flagship products — the Both-Side Spindle Drilling Machine and the Multi Wire Sawing Machine — represent years of dedicated engineering effort. These machines solve real production challenges: from reducing defect rates in silicon electrode machining to improving yield in hard brittle material slicing.

At Saehan Nanotech, we believe that precision is not just a specification — it is our promise. Every machine we build carries our commitment to quality, reliability, and the kind of after-service that builds long-term partnerships.

We continue to invest in R&D to meet the ever-evolving demands of the semiconductor industry, and we look forward to growing together with our customers worldwide.

An Guk-jin
CEO, Saehan Nanotech Co., Ltd.
